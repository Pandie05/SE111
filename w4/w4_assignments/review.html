Howl's Moving Castle

HOME CAST REVIEW

Review

Hendry Reyes | January 29, 2024

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Howl's Moving Catle is one of my favorite Studio Ghibli films of all time. I remember watching Ponyo (another Studio Ghibli film)
and really enjoying it, so after my girlfriend suggested this movie, I was very excited to watch it. My first watch through of the film,
I instantly fell in love with the characters, visuals, and especially the music. The characters all have good development and stories
that make them instantly memorable. The visuals just like all Studio Ghibli movies, are stunning, everything is animated with such and life and emotion
that words can't do them justice on this review alone. The music in Howl's Moving Castle is instantly a hit, with the song "Merry Go Round of Life"
being the main track of the film (it's so good I learned it on piano :p).
I think this movie is a great watch if you're just getting into Studio Ghibli or if you're just looking for a great overall movie!
